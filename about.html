Why Wave Energy Harvesting?

Our project explores harnessing the enormous, renewable power of ocean waves to generate electricity. With the ever-growing demand for clean energy, we saw an incredible opportunity in tapping into this abundant resource.

Project Vision:

To develop a sustainable, scalable, and cost-effective system that converts the kinetic energy of waves into electricity, providing reliable power for coastal communities and industries.

Problem Statement

Coastal regions often face energy shortages and rely on non-renewable sources. Our challenge is to create a system that effectively harnesses wave energy while minimizing environmental impact and installation costs.

Research & Insights

Extensive research into existing wave energy technologies revealed key challenges such as variable wave intensity, structural durability, and maintenance costs. Our design aims to address these issues with innovative engineering and sustainable practices.
