Hybrid Energy Harvesting System

Combining the power of ocean waves and the sun, our system offers a sustainable and reliable energy solution.

Overview

Our innovative solution integrates wave energy converters with solar panels to harness the kinetic energy of ocean waves along with abundant solar power during the day. This hybrid approach is designed to provide consistent energy output for coastal communities and industries.

Working Principle:

A floating platform equipped with a wave energy converter captures vertical motion, driving a small generator to produce electricity. In tandem, integrated solar panels boost energy production during sunny periods.

Key Features

  • Dual-source renewable energy capture
  • Modular and scalable design
  • Eco-friendly construction with low maintenance
  • Optimized for coastal and remote installations

Benefits & Impact

Our system reduces reliance on fossil fuels, lowers operational costs, and minimizes environmental impact. It offers a sustainable energy solution adaptable to various settings.

Future Enhancements

Future improvements include advanced AI for predictive maintenance, enhanced energy storage solutions, and scalable deployment strategies to further optimize system performance.
